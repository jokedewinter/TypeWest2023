About Joke De Winter

Joke is a Belgian graphic designer based in Woodhouse Eaves, United Kingdom. She started as a photographer and then became a self-employed web designer. After an intensive graphic design course she discovered a love for type design. Outside work she is a keen gardener and looking forward to the summer.
